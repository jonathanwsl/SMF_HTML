07 — Module Cards (Step 7 — Real Content, Unchanged from Original Map)
M1 · Account Development · Decision Support ★ Primary Fn1
AI-Augmented Account Prioritisation & Prospect Research
Module Description
Four accounts in your pipeline look similar on paper — same deal size, different buying signals, timelines, and relationship depth. Today's focus: using generative AI to score your account list against your own ICP criteria so lead generation effort goes to the right accounts.
Stage 3 Document
Target account list (Excel/CSV — company names, industry, size, contact names, existing notes)
Human Checkpoint
Does Claude's account tier ranking match your commercial intuition about buyer readiness? Where it diverges — which signal is Claude missing that you carry in your head?
M2 · Account Development · Decision Support
AI-Augmented Pipeline Health & Lead Prioritisation Review
Module Description
Your pipeline looks healthy by deal count — but average deal age is rising and momentum is harder to read. Today's focus: using generative AI to surface which leads are stalling, which are progressing, and which need a qualification decision before they age out.
Stage 3 Document
Inbound lead form responses or marketing-qualified lead list (Excel/CSV export)
Human Checkpoint
Are the disqualification flags Claude surfaces genuine deal-breakers — or edge cases your sales experience would override with more context?
M3 · Account Development · Decision Support
AI-Augmented Competitive Intelligence & Battle Card Drafting
Module Description
A competitor just won a deal in your key vertical — is this a pattern or an isolated loss? Today's focus: using generative AI to research the competitive landscape, surface win/loss signals, and draft a battle card.
Stage 3 Document
Competitor website printout, RFP with competitor mentions, or loss debrief notes (PDF/Word)
Human Checkpoint
Is the battle card's win-theme specific enough to use in a live sales conversation — or has it abstracted away the nuance that only experience provides?
M4 · Account Development · Skills Aug.
AI-Augmented Account Research & Re-Engagement Briefing
Module Description
A warm prospect went cold after a positive first call — no reply, no reason, no signal. Today's focus: using generative AI to research the account, surface what may have changed, and draft a re-engagement brief.
Stage 3 Document
Annual report, LinkedIn company page printout, or recent news articles on target account (PDF/Word)
Human Checkpoint
Does the briefing capture the strategic pressure the buyer's leadership is actually under — or only what's publicly stated and safely generic?
M5 · Account Development · Skills Aug.
AI-Augmented Pipeline Narrative & Outreach Sequencing
Module Description
A prospect has engaged but never replied — and your pipeline is full of accounts in the same limbo. Today's focus: using generative AI to draft a sequenced outreach plan that turns ambiguous engagement signals into a structured next-step narrative.
Stage 3 Document
Deal list with status, value, activity notes, and next steps (Excel/Word — no CRM integration required)
Human Checkpoint
Is the AI pipeline narrative telling the story leadership wants to hear — or the story the data actually supports? Where do they diverge?
M6 · Proposal Excellence · Skills Aug.
AI-Augmented Proposal & RFP Response Drafting
Module Description
An RFP lands with a 48-hour turnaround — not enough time to write well, too important to ignore. Today's focus: using generative AI to draft a structured proposal response that covers the brief, positions your solution, and gets to a reviewable first draft before the deadline.
Stage 3 Document
RFP document or client brief (Word/PDF)
Human Checkpoint
Would a senior bid manager sign off this draft? Identify one section where AI has over-generalised and the human version must be more specific.
M7 · Proposal Excellence · Skills Aug.
AI-Augmented Executive Summary & Value Proposition Writing
Module Description
Your executive sponsor just changed mid-deal — the new stakeholder doesn't know your solution and the proposal you sent no longer lands the same way. Today's focus: using generative AI to rewrite the executive summary and value proposition for a different audience.
Stage 3 Document
Discovery call notes or previous meeting summary (Word/PDF)
Human Checkpoint
Does the executive summary reflect this client's actual priorities — or the priorities any company in that industry would generically have?
M8 · Proposal Excellence · Decision Support
AI-Augmented Meeting Preparation & Discovery Call Brief
Module Description
A proposal has been silent for two weeks and the next conversation needs to move it forward or close it out. Today's focus: using generative AI to prepare a discovery call brief that surfaces what's really stalling the deal.
Stage 3 Document
Last meeting notes and company background email thread (Word/PDF)
Human Checkpoint
Has the AI call brief identified the one question whose answer would change your entire approach — or is it producing standard discovery questions any rep would ask?
