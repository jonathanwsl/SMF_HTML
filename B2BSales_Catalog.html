Applied Gen AI for B2B Sales
B2B sales professionals are already using AI — informally, inconsistently, and without a shared standard. This programme exists to close that gap. Each module addresses one real task in your sales workflow — the moment where informal AI use is costing you consistency, credibility, or deals. No coding required. No prior AI experience needed.
Prospect & Qualify
Each module is a complete 1-day programme addressing Prospect & Qualify Stage. Pick the task most relevant to your role right now — each stands alone and is independently assessed.
AI-Augmented Account ICP Scoring
7 Hours · Prospect & Qualify · 1 of 5
Score the list. Cut the noise. Call the right accounts.
Score a real prospect list against your own ICP criteria — stop spending quota capacity on accounts that look active but won't close.
  • Account scoring — AI ranks your prospect list against your ICP criteria and flags priority targets with rationale. Review, override based on relationship history, and commit to the accounts that matter.
  • ICP scoring workflow — AI specifies a reusable workflow with trigger, scoring logic and exception gate. Define the rules once before any outreach fires.
  • Workflow specification — walk away with a 5-field AI workflow brief — a non-technical specification ready to start the conversation with IT or a vendor.
