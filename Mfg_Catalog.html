AI Tools for Manufacturing — Singapore: 9 module marketing cards organised by Plan and Make functions

AI Tools for Manufacturing — Singapore
Manufacturing professionals across Singapore are already using AI — informally, inconsistently, and without a shared standard. This programme closes that gap. Each module addresses one real task in your planning or operations workflow — the moment where working without AI is costing you accuracy, response time, or uptime. No coding required. No prior AI experience needed.
Plan Function · Production Planning & Supply Chain
Each module is a complete 1-day programme addressing the Plan function. Pick the planning task most relevant to your role right now — each stands alone and is independently assessed.
AI-Augmented Demand Forecasting & S&OP Preparation
7 Hours · Plan · 1 of 5
Stop planning from last year's numbers. Start the cycle with signals.
Turn 12 months of demand data into a structured S&OP assumption brief — so the next planning cycle starts with signals, not gut feel.
S&OP assumption brief — AI analyses a 12-month demand dataset and drafts a 3-bullet brief covering trend signal, risk flag, and planning recommendation. Accept, reject, or modify each assumption before it enters the planning cycle.
Demand pattern validation — AI applies the ready prompt to your own demand data and surfaces seasonal patterns and anomalies for the next quarter. You determine which signals reflect real business conditions before the brief is finalised.
Workflow specification — walk away with a 5-field AI workflow brief — a non-technical specification ready to start the conversation with IT or a vendor.
